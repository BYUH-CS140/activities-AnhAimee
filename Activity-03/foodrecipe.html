Spring roll

Ingredients

  1. Rice paper wrappers
  2. Vermicelli noodles
  3. Shrimp
  4. cabbage
  5. Carrots
  6. Fresh herb

Steps

  1. Soak rice paper.
  2. Layer noodles, veggies, and shrimp.
  3. Roll tightly.
  4. Serve with dipping sauce.
